Why use ShareTrace?

Contact tracing is key to the fight against the pandemic - but it is not enough.

We help individuals and organisations understand their risk of exposure to Covid-19 with a clear and dynamic interface.

Gain the security to get back to work and social life.

Ethical, Anonymous, User-controlled

Our users own their data, because Sharetrace stores what it needs in Personal Data Accounts (PDAs) owned and controlled by each user. Learn more about PDAs

ShareTrace can compute private risk scores and network risk models for them, all privately and anonymously. No data is stored by ShareTrace.

YOUR SOCIAL EXPOSURE SCORE: PROTECT YOUR COMMUNITY

Using the risk updates provided to the SafeTrace app by our anonymous network graph model, users can understand how likely they are to have met an infected person, or themselves to be asymptomatic carriers of Covid-19. With this knowledge, they can better protect their communities
